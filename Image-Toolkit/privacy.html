Privacy Policy for Image Toolkit

Last updated: June 2026

Image Toolkit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our Chrome Extension.

1. No Data Collection (Local Processing)

Image Toolkit is a 100% local, client-side browser extension. We DO NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, images, files, or browsing history. All image manipulation, including conversion, compression, watermarking, cropping, and EXIF processing, is executed entirely within your local browser sandbox using HTML5 Canvas APIs.

2. Use of Permissions

  • storage: Used solely to save your local UI preferences (e.g., theme settings, watermark presets) on your own device.
  • activeTab & scripting: Used only when you explicitly interact with the extension to process an image on the current active page.

3. Third-Party Services

This extension does not connect to any external APIs, servers, or trackers. Your data never leaves your computer.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our developer support channel.

Image Toolkit 隐私权政策

最后更新日期：2026年6月

Image Toolkit（“我们”）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策阐明了我们通过 Chrome 扩展程序处理信息的规范。

1. 无数据收集（纯本地处理）

Image Toolkit 是一款 100% 本地运行的客户端浏览器扩展。我们不会收集、存储、传输或共享您的任何个人数据、图片、文件或浏览历史。 所有图片操作（包括转换、压缩、水印、裁剪、旋转和 EXIF 处理）均完全在您的本地浏览器沙盒内（利用 HTML5 Canvas 技术）执行。

2. 权限的使用说明

  • storage： 仅用于在您自己的设备上保存本地 UI 偏好设置（如暗黑模式切换、水印预设）。
  • activeTab & scripting： 仅在您明确点击并与扩展程序交互时，用于处理当前活动页面上的图片。

3. 第三方服务

本扩展程序不连接任何外部 API、服务器或追踪器。您的数据绝不会离开您的电脑。

4. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过开发者支持渠道与我们联系。
