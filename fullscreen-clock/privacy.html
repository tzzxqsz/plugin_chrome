Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our Full-Screen Clock Chrome Extension (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") handles user data. We value your privacy immensely.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension DOES NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal identity information or browsing data.

We request the storage permission solely to save your customized clock preferences (such as background colors, text colors, and display modes) locally in your browser. This data stays entirely on your local device and is never uploaded to any remote servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not contain any third-party advertisements, trackers, or analytics code, and does not share any information with third parties.

3. Permission Justification

Storage Permission: Used exclusively to remember your personalized themes and clock configurations to provide a seamless user experience.

4. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. Your continued use of the Extension constitutes your agreement to this policy.
