Privacy Policy for UUID Generator

Last Updated: May 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the "UUID Generator" Chrome extension (the "Extension").

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or user information. All UUID generation happens locally within your browser using secure, built-in cryptographic functions.

2. Permissions Disclosures

The Extension requests the following permission, which is strictly used for its core functionality:

  • clipboardWrite: Used exclusively to copy the generated UUIDs to your system clipboard when you click the copy button. We do not read your clipboard data under any circumstances.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and the version number of the extension will be updated accordingly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Extension, please contact us via the developer communication channels on the Chrome Web Store.
