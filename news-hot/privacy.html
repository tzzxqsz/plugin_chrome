Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with a "privacy-first" approach, ensuring your data remains yours.

Last Updated: May 2026

Extension Overview

This extension provides real-time trending news and hot topic information. Our primary goal is to deliver content efficiently while maintaining the highest standards of user privacy.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). Your browsing activity remains completely private.

User Data

No user account, password, browsing history, or personal information is collected. We do not track who you are or what you browse outside of the extension's necessary functions.

Permissions Usage

We only request permissions strictly necessary for functionality.

storage

Used to save local user settings and preferences. Data stays on your device.

alarms

Used to periodically refresh news data in the background to keep content fresh.

tabs

Used to open news links selected by the user in a new browser tab.

Remote Data

The extension retrieves public news and trending topic information from remote API services. This connection is secure and only fetches public content.

Third-Party Services

The extension may access third-party news APIs and RSS feeds to display public news content. We do not share your personal data with these services.

Policy Changes & Contact

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the extension. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

If you have any questions, please contact the developer.
