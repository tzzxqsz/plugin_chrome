隐私权政策

本隐私权政策阐述了我们的扩展程序（以下简称“本插件”）如何处理用户数据。我们极其重视您的隐私。

1. 数据收集与使用

本插件不会收集、存储、传输或共享您的任何个人身份信息、测试文本或正则表达式。

所有正则表达式的匹配、测试和替换逻辑完全在您的本地浏览器中运行。我们没有建立任何外部服务器，您的数据绝不会离开您的设备。

2. 权限使用说明

  • storage（存储权限）： 仅用于在您本地的浏览器中（通过 chrome.storage.local）保存您的常用偏好设置、最近输入的测试历史记录，以便在您下次打开插件时恢复状态。这些数据绝不会被上传或分享。

3. 第三方服务

本插件不包含任何第三方分析工具、广告追踪器或外部 API 调用。

4. 变更通知

如果未来因功能升级需要变更隐私政策，我们会在合规的前提下更新此页面，并通过版本更新日志告知用户。

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") handles user data. We take your privacy very seriously.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension DOES NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal identifiable information, testing texts, or regular expressions.

All regex evaluation, testing, and text replacement logic are executed entirely within your local browser. We do not operate any external servers, and your data never leaves your device.

2. Permissions Justification

  • storage: Used solely to save your local preferences and recent testing history within your own browser (via chrome.storage.local) for a seamless user experience upon reopening. This data is never uploaded or shared.

3. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate any third-party analytics tools, advertisement trackers, or external API calls.

4. Changes to This Policy

Should this policy change in the future due to feature upgrades, we will update this page accordingly and notify users via extension update logs.
